CHINESE LANTERN FESTIVAL

Up close with 2,000 glowing sculptures at Philly’s Chinese Lantern Festival

ABOUT

The Philadelphia Chinese Lantern Festival in Franklin Square returns to Philadelphia’s Historic District May 1 – July 5, 2020 with handcrafted lanterns — created using traditional Chinese methods — that illuminate the park when the sun sets. Lantern designs in previous years have included sea creatures, lions, pandas and mythological creatures.

HISTORY

The Philadelphia Chinese Lantern Festival is a celebration of light and culture that features artisans from China and the Philadelphia region.The popular festival welcomed more than 90,000 people from all 50 states and 10 countries to Philly and Franklin Square when it debuted in 2016 for the first time.In 2018, more than 130,000 people from all 50 states and 20 countries experienced the festival.
